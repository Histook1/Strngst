Strongest App Story

In 2018, we brought the best talent in technology, hardware and production together to accomplish an ambitious goal: bring the community and excitement of boutique workout into the home. The idea struck us after years of struggling to get to the workout classes we loved, while balancing our demanding jobs and busy families. So we made it our mission to bring immersive and challenging workouts into people's lives in a more accessible, affordable and efficient way.
